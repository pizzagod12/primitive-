Primitive Skateboarding

Following Rodriguez's departure from Plan B, he produced a batch of 500 gold-colored skateboard decks for a one-off sale. Soon, Netkin presented the idea of "Primitive Skateboards" to Rodriguez. His gold bar decks sold out in 49 seconds and he had to add more so others that didn't get a chance to get one were able to get one.

Team

Rodriguez launched his own skateboard brand, Primitive Skateboarding, on April 10, 2014, with the initial team consisting of himself, Nick Tucker and Carlos Ribeiro. Now the Primitive Skateboarding team has 2 more pros which are Shane O'Neill and Bastien Salabanzi.

PRIMITIVE!

Rodriguez explained to the media: "To create my own board brand and have a chance to be a part of skateboarding beyond just being a pro skater is a dream come true." Primitive is now a worldwide known skateboarding brand, that consists of great flatground and street skateboarders. This is Primitive.

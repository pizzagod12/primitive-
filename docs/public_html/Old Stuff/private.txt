Rodriguez owns a private skateboard park in the Canoga Park area of California, U.S.[44] that features design elements from his sponsors, Mountain Dew, Nike SB, and Target.[45] Rodriguez designed the park with skateboard park builder Mike Roebke, who used a computer program to blend his own ideas with drawings from Rodriguez. Rodriguez has stated, "I enjoy skating there and I'm blessed to have that type of place at my fingertips."[8]

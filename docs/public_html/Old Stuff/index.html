Welcome to Marquan

So what is a Marquan? The short answer is that he comes from a person like everyone else. HE is THE creator of this website and we Quans hope he does well. A slightly longer version is that we're a collection of Quans that like doing stuff. During our travels around the country, we take time to document the cities we love. You'll also occasionally find articles on design about THE QUAN or photos of the places we like and been to.

What were not?

We aren't the type of people that like to steal people's photographs and call them our own. We give credit to any photgraphs we take. Also, we aren't professional phtographers, but we do take really really really good pictures.

We also occasionally have meetups. If, for example, we know we're coming to Miami, we'll let everyone know and have them meet with us a certain day and then we will talk to them about our website and photos, talk to the QUANS!
